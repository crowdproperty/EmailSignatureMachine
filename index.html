How to add your email signature to Outlook

Outlook Desktop (Windows / Mac)

  1. Open Outlook.
  2. Click New Email.
  3. In the new email window, click Signature → Signatures…
  4. Click New, name your signature (e.g. “CrowdProperty”), then click OK.
  5. Return here and click Copy Signature.
  6. Paste it into the signature editor (Ctrl+V / Cmd+V).
  7. Set it as default for:
    • New messages
    • Replies/forwards (optional)
  8. Click OK to save.

Outlook on the Web (New Outlook / Office 365)

  1. Open Outlook on the web.
  2. Click the Settings cog (top right).
  3. Go to Accounts in the left sidebar.
  4. Click Signatures.
  5. Create a New signature.
  6. Return here & click Copy Signature.
  7. Paste the signature into Outlook’s signature box.
  8. Set your defaults for:
    • New messages
    • Replies/forwards (optional)
  9. Click Save.
